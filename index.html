Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium mehndi services.

1. Where are you located in Greater Noida?

We are located at SHOP NO.31, Ground Floor, Eco Bazaar-1, Supertech Eco Village-1, Greater Noida West Road, Sector 1, Bisrakh Jalalpur, Greater Noida, UP 203206.

2. Do you provide home services for bridal mehndi?

Yes, we provide professional home visiting services for brides in Greater Noida, Noida Extension, and surrounding areas.

Absolutely. We use 100% natural, homemade organic henna powder mixed with premium essential oils. No harmful chemicals are ever used.

Yes! Because we use premium organic henna leaves, our mehndi yields a guaranteed rich, dark maroon stain when proper aftercare is followed.

For peak wedding seasons, we highly recommend booking 2-3 months in advance to secure your preferred date and time.
